  2. Who is Jesus?

    For centuries, people have wondered who Jesus Christ is. Whether you consider Him a myth, a historical figure, a prophet sent from God, or even the very God Himself, there is no denying Jesus' influence on human history. It would not be a strech to call Him the most influential figure of the Common Era.


    There is no dearth of literature, both contemporary and antiquated, on the person of Jesus Christ. Various people, scholars and laymen alike, have commented widely on this man, forming a broad spectrum of opinion regarding the life, work, and person of Jesus Christ. However, there is no better source or authority on Jesus than the Bible itself.
